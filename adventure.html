Adventure Activities

Sandboarding, 4x4 dune rides, mountain trekking, surfing, and island-hopping — Ilocos Norte is an adventurer's playground.

Get Active

Thrilling Experiences

From the vast sand dunes of Laoag to the highland trails of Adams, Ilocos Norte offers adventure at every level of intensity.

🏜️
La Paz Sand Dunes
All Levels · 4x4 Rides · Sandboarding

Ride across the largest sand dunes in the Philippines aboard a 4x4 vehicle, or try sandboarding down the slopes. An exhilarating desert experience unlike anything else in Southeast Asia.

🥾
Kabigan Falls Trek
Moderate · Trekking · Swimming

A 30-minute trek through lush forests and rice paddies leads to a stunning 35-meter waterfall. Swim in the cool natural pool at its base — one of the most rewarding hikes in Northern Luzon.

🏄
Surfing & Water Sports
Pagudpud · Nov–Feb Season
🧗
Adams Mountain Trek
Adams · Advanced Level
🤿
Snorkeling & Diving
Blue Lagoon · Pagudpud

Pricing

4x4 Dune Rides: ₱500–₱1,500/ride
Sandboard Rental: ₱100–₱200
Kabigan Falls Trek: ₱50 guide fee
Surfing Lessons: ₱500–₱800/session

Best Time to Visit

Sand Dunes: Year-round (early morning best)
Trekking: Nov–May (dry season)
Surfing: November–February
Waterfall: Year-round

Safety Tips

Always hire certified local guides for mountain treks. Wear protective eyewear on the dunes. Bring sufficient water, sunscreen, and first-aid basics for all outdoor activities.
