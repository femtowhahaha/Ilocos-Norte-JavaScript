Ilocos Norte offers accommodations for every budget — from luxury beachfront resorts to charming heritage inns and budget guesthouses.

Hannah's Beach Resort
Saud Beach, Pagudpud

Wake up to the sound of waves at this beloved beachfront resort on Saud Beach. Comfortable cottages, a beachside restaurant serving fresh seafood, and direct access to the white sand shore.

★★★★☆
Blue Lagoon Beach Resort
Pagudpud, Ilocos Norte

An eco-friendly resort perched beside the stunning Blue Lagoon cove. Elevated cottages with sea views, snorkeling equipment, and guided nature tours make this a nature lover's paradise.

★★★☆☆
Fort Ilocandia Resort Hotel
Calayab Beach, Laoag

A landmark resort hotel near Laoag City featuring extensive grounds, a golf course, multiple dining options, and a private beach — one of the most historically significant resort hotels in Northern Luzon.

₱4,500 / night
Book Now
★★★☆☆
Museo de Ilocos Norte Inn
Batac City, Ilocos Norte

A charming heritage-themed boutique inn in Batac City — decorated with local artwork and antiques, offering an authentic window into Ilocano culture. Within walking distance of major historic sites.

₱1,200 / night
Book Now

Windmill Guesthouse
Bangui, Ilocos Norte

A budget-friendly guesthouse in Bangui municipality — clean rooms, friendly staff, and an unbeatable location within walking distance of the iconic Bangui Windmills. Perfect for the budget-conscious traveler.
