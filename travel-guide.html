Climate

When to Visit

Dry Season (NovMay)

The ideal time to visit. Expect sunny skies, calm seas, and comfortable temperatures (2432C). Beach activities, festivals, and outdoor adventures are at their best.

Recommended for Tourists
Wet Season (Jun-Oct)

Typhoon season brings heavy rainfall and rough seas. Some roads may flood. However, landscapes are lush and green, and accommodation prices are lower.

Typhoon Risk
Itineraries

Suggested Itineraries

3-Day Highlights Tour

1

Day 1 Laoag City & Heritage

Arrive at Laoag Airport Saint William Cathedral & Sinking Bell Tower La Paz Sand Dunes (4x4 ride) Empanada at the Riverside Laoag City dinner

2

Day 2 Paoay & Windmills

Paoay Church (UNESCO) Paoay Lake Lunch at Batac (Marcos Mausoleum) Bangui Windmills at sunset Overnight in Pagudpud

3

Day 3 Pagudpud Paradise

Saud Beach morning swim Kabigan Falls trek Blue Lagoon Kapurpurawan Rock Formation Patapat Viaduct Return to Laoag

5-Day Complete Explorer

Includes all 3-day highlights PLUS: Day 4 Adams Municipality trek to Timmangtang Rock and waterfalls. Day 5 Laoag food tour (empanada, longganisa, bagnet), souvenir shopping at pasalubong centers, and departure.

What to Pack

  • Lightweight, breathable clothing
  • Strong sunscreen (SPF 50+)
  • Insect repellent for forest hikes
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Rain jacket (wet season)
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Cash (limited ATMs in rural areas)
  • Power bank
